train app

user-centered design challenge

The Mobile Transport App is a UI/UX project designed to enhance the travel experience. This app addresses key user needs, including real-time updates, ticket purchasing, minimalistic design, and a rewards system.

The project began with creating paper prototypes based on fixed personas, followed by iterative improvements guided by feedback. I focused on simplifying the interface and ensuring ease of navigation. This project was a valuable experience in user-centered design and iterative testing, culminating in a refined, user-friendly prototype.
