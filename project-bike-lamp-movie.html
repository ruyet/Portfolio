bike lamp movie

a creative documentary project about finding a lost bike lamp.

watch movie

Bike Lamp is a mockumentary-style video project about a missing bike lamp, where I worked on planning, filming, and editing. We started with a storyboard and script, then filmed the scenes using a custom-designed background setup to make the interviews look realistic.

I handled most of the editing, first learning DaVinci Resolve for video cuts and color grading, then switching to After Effects for sound, transitions, and an animated title reveal. I also experimented with visual effects like crime-scene photos and dramatic lighting to enhance the story.

This project taught me how to think ahead while filming, edit with intention, and use visual details to support storytelling. It was a fun but technical project that challenged both my creativity and editing workflow.
