about me

who am I?

I'm a 24-year-old ICT & Media Design student with a passion for user-centered design and front-end development. I have a background in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. My creative side started early, with graphic design, photo editing, and video projects, and that visual foundation still influences the way I think about digital products.

I'm now focusing more on UX design by learning how people interact with digital products and how to design experiences that feel natural and easy to use. My goal is to become a UX designer who brings together clean visuals and practical, user-focused thinking.
